Universities in Poland - FAQ

This site is designed to help students choose a university in Poland. Here you can find information about universities, their ratings, and reviews from students.

If your university is not listed on the website, please email us. You can find the contact details on the Contact page.

To rate a university, you need to register and log in. After that, you can rate the university on a scale from 1 to 5.

To leave a review, you need to register and log in. After that, you can write a review in the comments section.

To contact the site administration, you can send an email to the address provided in the Contact section.

Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us at 124507@student.san.edu.pl
